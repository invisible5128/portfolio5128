Fitness App

A Fitness App to keep track of your Fitness.

About App

Android App built using Flutter. The App keeps track of the Fitness data collected from Fitness bands.

APP features

  • Used Google Fit Api to connect to various Fitness Bands.
  • Keeps track of various Fitness Data including: Calories Burned, Step count and Heart Rate.
  • Keeps track of the Heart Rate and notifies the user of any unusual activity as High Heart Rate.

Screenshots of the App

The App was developed in Flutter Framework. Keeps track of Real Time Fitness information from various Fitness Bands.
